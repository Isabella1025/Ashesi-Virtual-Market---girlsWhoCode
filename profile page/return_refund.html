Returns & Refunds

Our Returns & Refunds Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 30 days for a refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition and packaging.

Steps to Return:

  • Contact our support team to request a return authorization.
  • Package the item securely, including all original packaging and accessories.
  • Send the item to the address provided in the return authorization.

Refund Process:

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after the return is received and inspected. You will receive the refund through the original payment method.

For assistance, email us at support@example.com.
